LastHalo Button

LastHalo is steadily gaining popularity among bloggers. We would like to thank each and every LastHalo users for your support.

We're happy and strongly encouraged by LastHalo's response and we promise to keep on improving the script.

By the way, we have created a small little button which you can display on your side to show a little support for us and as a way to say 'thanks' to us for developing and hosting this add-on.

Although there is already a link back to us on the Javascript add-on, we would be happy if you could provide us an extra link back to us with the form of a button we've just created or as a plain text link in your blog template. Human surfers may have noticed the link on the Javascript add-on as evident by the gaining popularity of LastHalo, search engines' spiders such as Googlebot by Google would ignore Javascript add-on, thus disabling us from benefiting from the link back from your blog.

Once again, we would really appreciate it if new and existing users alike could do us a small favour by linking back to us. The following code will display a small button on your blog that links to us.

<a href="http://lasthalo.uzyn.com">
<img src="http://lasthalo.uzyn.com/lhicon.gif" width="88" height="31" border="0" alt="Last Comments Add-On by LastHalo" / >
</a>




Thank you very much for your support!

For new users, please refer to the simple LastHalo installation instructions.
